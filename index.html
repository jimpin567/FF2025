Equal-time Rotation (Bench-Group Subs)

Bench count = roster − on-field. Those players sit together and groups cycle. Active positions also rotate so everyone gets QB.

Game Management

  • Series flow: Same series plays both ways. On change of possession, tap Next ▶ to sub the next bench group.
  • Landmarks: cones at 5 & 10 = depth cues for kids.
  • Mirror: switch “Mirror” to flip the play left/right for hash or strong-hand kids.
  • Simple cadence: “Huddle — Play Name — On GO.”
