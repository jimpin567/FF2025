Base Youth Defenses

Keep it simple. Priorities: pull the flag, protect the goal line, eyes on hips (not ball), rally to the ball.

Practical Game Management

  • Sideline cadence: “Huddle here” → play name → “On GO” → “Series X on” → line up by diagram.
  • Default rhythm: 1st & 2nd down Slants/Flood, 3rd down Smash, 4th down Go or Flood.
  • Time saver: Teach WR landmarks (outside numbers, 5 and 10 yards) with cones.
  • Parent transparency: The rotation table is math, not politics. Everyone plays QB once per cycle.

60‑minute Practice

  1. Warm‑up & flags — 5
  2. Routes on air (slant, hitch, go) — 10
  3. Install 3 plays (Smash/Flood/Crossers) — 12
  4. QB/RB timing + jet sweep handoff — 10
  5. Team vs air (script 9 plays) — 18
  6. Two‑minute drill — 4
  7. Huddle & parent note — 1
