Flag Football Coaching Kit — Simple, Fair, Fun

Roster of 8–9 players. 6 on field. Age 8–9. Concepts: Go, Slant, Smash, Flood.
QB Center (snap) WR Left / Right Slot RB

Field is vertical: Offense at bottom going up. Routes animate to show direction.

Play 1: Double Go (2 verts) — "Green"

Easy read: throw to the first WR who runs past their defender. Slot runs short out as a safety valve.
  • Count “one-one-thousand, two-one-thousand” then throw.
  • Coaching: “Eyes downfield, feet set, front shoulder to target.”
  • Progression: WR-L or WR-R deep; if covered, Slot out; last, RB checkdown.

Play 2: Quick Slants — "Blue"

Ball out fast. Great vs. soft zones. Center sits for a quick settle route.
  • QB pre-snap: pick left or right based on cushion.
  • Three-step rhythm throw. If closed, Center settle.
  • Call: “Blue — Slants. On Go.”

Play 3: Smash (Corner + Hitch) — "Gold"

Hi/low read on one side. Outside runs hitch, inside runs corner.
  • Read corner defender: if he sinks, throw hitch; if he jumps hitch, throw corner.
  • Teaching: show “hi/low” using cones.

Play 4: Flood (3-level to one side) — "White"

Overload one sideline with short, mid, deep. QB rolls that way.
  • QB rolls right; read short → mid → deep on the move.
  • If defense over-pursues, hit the backside drag.

Rotation Generator (Equal Time, Everyone Plays Every Spot)

Number players 1–9. We field 6 positions each series: QB, C, WR-L, WR-R, Slot, RB. Over N = roster size series, each player benches N - 6 times and plays QB exactly once. Default series count equals roster size for perfect balance.

Sideline Commands (keep it simple)

  • “Huddle here.” Knees bent, listen.
  • “Blue — Slants. On Go.” Repeat the call back.
  • “Line up!” Center on ball, WRs on numbers, Slot next to tackle box, RB offset.
  • “Ready — Go!” Snap on “Go.”
  • “Next group: 1–6 on, others bench.” Use rotation chart every series.

Keep cadence consistent. Use hand signals if crowd is loud.

Position Reminders for Kids

  • QB: two hands on ball, shoulders to target, decide fast.
  • Center: clean snap then turn and look for quick pass.
  • WR-L/WR-R: start fast, crisp cuts, look back early.
  • Slot: be the safety valve — own the short area.
  • RB: fake first, then leak to flat; show hands.

Why This Fair System Works

  • Equal reps: across N series, every kid plays QB once and benches exactly N - 6 times.
  • Zero politics: assignments are math-driven, not coach preference.
  • Faster practices: kids learn by repeating the same simple roles in rotation.
  • Less friction with parents: published plan, equal time, clear logic.

Practice Plan (60 minutes)

  1. Dynamic warm-up & flags check — 5 min
  2. Routes on air (Slant, Hitch, Go) — 10 min
  3. Play install on cones (Smash right, Flood right) — 10 min
  4. QB/RB hand & throw reps (timing) — 10 min
  5. Team vs air: script 8 plays, rotate each series — 15 min
  6. Competition: two-minute drill — 8 min
  7. Huddle review & parent summary — 2 min
